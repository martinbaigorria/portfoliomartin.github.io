About Me

Martin Baigorria

My name is Martin Baigorria, I am 24 years old, and I am from Buenos Aires, Argentina. For over 2 years, I have immersed myself in the world of programming and dedicated time to self-study, developing skills in logic and problem-solving. During the past year, in addition to that experience, I have had the privilege of working in an outsourced company for the Argentine National Army, Iscoders, where I acquired valuable skills in SQL Server Management, ASP.Net, MVC, C#, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript development, while also learning about agile methodologies and the importance of teamwork.

Working

I work as a Systems Developer for the Argentine Army, where I am involved in making modifications to both Back-End and Front-End code. In my first stage, I worked as a database administrator for the Army's Accounting Department, resolving support issues related to their systems, both in Back-End and Front-End, using C# .Net and SQL Server Management. After a year of working, I received a promotion and now I am involved in larger national-level projects. I work on development tasks for Military Hospitals and Military Schools (Liceos Militares) across the country, providing support and development using the same mentioned languages. The team follows an organized structure using JIRA with daily meetings to handle and develop new updates for the websites of Hospitals and Liceos. My role is Full Stack, handling tasks related to the database, Front-End, and Back-End.
